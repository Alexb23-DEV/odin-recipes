Juicy Lemon Pepper Wings

An amazing Lemon Pepper wings recipe that will for sure "make your back big" and have anyone you make it for come running back

These lemon pepper wings baked in the oven are a real crowd-pleaser: savory, simple, and fresh!

Ingredients

  • 6 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons coarse sea salt
  • 2 tablespoons ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of onion powder
  • 2 tablespoons of garlic powder
  • 1 (3 pound) bag organic chicken wings
  • 2 tablespoon unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Season the wings with the olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic, and onion powder
  3. Whisk olive oil, lemon zest, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl; add wings and toss to coat. Spread coated wings in a single layer onto the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven at 400 degrees, insert wings for about 35/50 minutes(Until very cripsy)
  5. Serve hot
